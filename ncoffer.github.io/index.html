Nia Coffer


When I was a baby I always moved my feet. To no surprise, I took my first dance class and was hooked. I have been dancing ever since. Yet, I could not have imagined all of the amazing opportunities I would have in the future. As I grew up, I took up different forms of art including painting, drawing, pottery, and photography. Taking classes or practicing on weekends I formed new islands of myself, ones I can never live without. Soon many more doors opened as I started high school. I began to find a love for musical theatre and even coding. While dancing year-round, I became a part of my school's ambassador program, musical theatre program, dance program, and even took a year long photography class while keeping up my studies. Throughout my years of schooling I began to form a particular liking in math, history, and english.
